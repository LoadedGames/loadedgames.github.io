Dramatown


Create discord; destroy lives.

Project Description

Dramatown is a single-player game in which the player is encouraged to engage in their cynicism and dark sense of humour. The player is faced with the day to day suburban lives of the people in an impossibly idyllic small American town. In any way they see fit, they can utilise the wide range of tools they’re given to create rifts and cracks in the relationships and livelihoods of these people. Violence is never an answer as that would be too boring and there’s far better ways to have fun. If you’ve ever tried to ruin the lives of your characters in The Sims, or wished you could be that contestant on reality TV shows causes drama just to watch the world burn, this game is for you.

Features:

  • Play as either Carl or Caroline, two cunning cynics who want to cause pandemonium in town for their own enjoyment.
  • Talk to and manipulate the quirky inhabitants of the town to find out their weaknesses and get them to do your bidding.
  • Explore the town in a number of levels.
  • Sneak into restricted areas .
  • Pick up and interact with household objects in a mischieveous manner.
  • Create mayhem with a method of your choosing, and replay levels to find out the many other ways you could have done so.

Details:

  • Dramatown is being developed for PC, Mac, and Linux.
  • A free demo will be released on digital storefronts and on this page in 2019.
